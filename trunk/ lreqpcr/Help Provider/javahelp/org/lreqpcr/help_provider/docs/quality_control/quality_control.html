Quality Control Overview

One of the distinctive aspects of LRE is the ability to implement various quality control methodologies. These encompass fundamental issues of optical precision and monitoring of assay performance, to verification of quantitative accuracy and anomalies that occur with target quantities below ten molecules, to kinetic anomalies produced by some enzyme formulations, amplicons and samples.

Although as a whole such issues may appear to increase the complexity of conducting qPCR, in practice these methodologies can provide an unprecedented level of insight that is difficult, if not impossible to achieve with conventional methods.
