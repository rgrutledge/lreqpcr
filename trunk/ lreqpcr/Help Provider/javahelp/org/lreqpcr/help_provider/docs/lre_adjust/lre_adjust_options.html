Options for Adjusting LRE Analysis

Two options are available for adjusting how the LRE analysis is conducted. Both compensate for aberrancies that can impact quantitative precision.

The first addresses the propensity of Qiagen QuantiTect enzyme formulation to generate overestimations of amplification efficiency by fixing Emax to 100%.

The second addresses apparent variances in fluorescence signal intensity, the most prominent being well-to-well differences in the fluorescence units generated by the instrument's optical system, and is referred to as Fmax normalization.
