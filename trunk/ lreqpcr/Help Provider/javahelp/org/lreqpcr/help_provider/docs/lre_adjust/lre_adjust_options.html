Options for Adjusting LRE Analysis

Two options are available for adjusting how the LRE analysis is conducted.

The first is the propensity of Qiagen QuantiTect enzyme formulation to generate overestimations of amplification efficiency. Although the underlying mechanism is unknown, fixing Emax to 100% has been found to be effective in reducing the biases caused by this kinetic aberrancy.

The second addresses variances in fluorescence signal intensity, the most prominent being well-to-well differences in the fluorescence scale generated by an instrument's optical system, and is referred to as Fmax normalization.
