Options for Adjusting LRE Analysis

Two options are available for adjusting how the LRE analysis is conducted. Both involve normalization that compensates for two potential aberrancies that can impact quantitative accuracy.

The first addresses the propensity of Qiagen Quantitect enzyme formulation to generate overestimations of amplification efficiency by fixing Emax to 100%, and is referred to as Emax Normalization.

The second addresses qPCR instrument variance in fluorescence signal calibration, which can generate quantitative errors due to well-to-well differences in the fluorescence units generated by the instrument's optical system, and is referred to as Fmax normalization.
