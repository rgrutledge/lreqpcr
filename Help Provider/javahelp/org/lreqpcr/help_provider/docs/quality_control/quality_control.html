Quality Control Overview

One of the distinctive aspects of LRE qPCR are the quality control capabilities it provides. Most of these were implemented during the development and testing of the LRE Analyzer, and address such issues such as monitoring assay performance and verification of quantitative accuracy, in addition to revealing kinetic anomalies produced by some enzyme formulations, amplicons and samples.
